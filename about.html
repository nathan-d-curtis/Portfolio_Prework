My Background

I was born and raised in Orange County, California where I grew up in a single-parent home after the age of 11. So, I became the "man of the house", I guess you could say. That experience probably changed me more than I realize. I do know it taught me to work hard and to keep on learning. In school, I always had an interest in computers. From the original Mac I would use at my friend's house. To the Apple IIe that I learned Basic and Pascal on. Then, years later, I worked for a childhood friend and built PC's from scratch. I learned so much and still use that knowledge to this day. I should have gone into development at that time, but for one reason or another, I didn't. But, as I now tell my kiddos, it's never too late. If you have breath in your lungs, you can learn new things. So, here I am. Ready to change my life, as well as my family's.

This is me.


Reinventing myself, once again.
